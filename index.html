2. Decision boundary and handoff

HCB analyses and recommends; MetLife decides and communicates.

HCB delivery team - analysis and recommendation
Review claim file, medical, occupational, financial and policy evidence.
Apply agreed MetLife process, country guidance and recommendation template.
Identify risks, inconsistencies, evidence gaps and escalation triggers.
Produce structured recommendation and contemporaneous file note.
HANDOFF
MetLife - decision and customer ownership
Review HCB recommendation and supporting analysis.
Approve, decline, request further information or redirect strategy.
Retain final claim authority and regulatory correspondence.
Communicate with policyholder / member through agreed channels.
